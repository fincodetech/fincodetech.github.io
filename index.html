Nidhi Company Operation

 A Nidhi Company is a type of company in the Indian non-banking finance sector, recognized under section 406 of the Companies Act, 2013. Their core business is borrowing and lending money between their members. The main purpose is to increase the habit of thrift among various sector of the economy.

Increased Statutory Compliances

A Nidhi Company is governed by Ministry of Corporate Affair having separate set of rules for dos and don'ts under the Nidhi Business. Hence there are lot of legal compliances to be taken care to legally run the Business in a proper and effective manner.

How To Run the Nidhi in Profit

 Day to Day management of Nidhi Activities in such a manner that will enable the Promoter to track the Interest Earning/Payout Activities viz a viz to achieve or enlarge the Profit from Nidhi Business.
